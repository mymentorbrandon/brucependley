About Dr. Pendley

The Physician Behind
In Good Health

Dr. Bruce Pendley has spent more than three decades doing what he loves most: caring for patients and families across every stage of life. As the Medical Director of In Good Health, he brings the kind of steady, experienced leadership that gives both patients and clinical staff confidence that they are in good hands.

His academic foundation is exceptional. He earned his Chemistry degree from Emory University in Atlanta in 1983—a rigorous scientific grounding that would define his evidence-based approach to medicine—before going on to complete his MD at the Medical College of Georgia in Augusta in 1987.

Following medical school, Dr. Pendley completed his Family Practice Residency in Rome, Georgia (1987–1990), emerging as a board-certified physician through the American Academy of Family Physicians (AAFP). His specialty training in Hospice and Palliative Care gave him a profound, irreplaceable perspective on the human side of medicine—one that continues to shape how In Good Health approaches every patient interaction.

Today, Dr. Pendley works closely alongside Dr. Laurie Davis, NP, who leads day-to-day patient care and consultations. Together, under the umbrella of AlwaysInGoodHealth.com, they offer patients a seamless, comprehensive experience grounded in trust, expertise, and genuine compassion.

Outside of medicine, Dr. Pendley is a proud husband and father of two sons. His love of gardening, beekeeping, and the natural world reflect the same patient, attentive care he brings to his practice—and his years as a Boy Scout and Scoutmaster speak to a lifelong commitment to service, integrity, and community.
